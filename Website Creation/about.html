about

At Hair We Are Salon, we are committed to helping you look and feel your best and we believe that healthy hair care is an essential part of a healthy lifestyle. Hair We Are Salon was founded in 2020 with the mission of providing hair care services with a professional but casual atmosphere.

Our team has over 20 years of experience in hair styling and cutting, we offer high quality service mixed with an ingenious focus on technique and attention to detail in a no fuss, relaxed environment. Our brand new salon has been designed to help make your stay not only as comfortable as possible but also friendly and welcoming.
